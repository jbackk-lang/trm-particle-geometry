   * **Helium** represents the fundamental, harmonious base-state of closed spatial symmetry.
   * **Iron** is the absolute geometric optimum ($\rho_{max}$ per node) where the topological field lines achieve maximum structural packaging efficiency before requiring geometric inversion.
3. **The Particle Turning Point:** When particles decay or collide, they do not "annihilate". The localized manifold opens, enters the **Turning Point**, and through the **TIMDR Self-Cleaning protocol**, sheds its non-local high-frequency noise, re-clustering the energy density into new stable geometric knot configurations (new particles).
